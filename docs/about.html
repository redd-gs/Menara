About Us

Menara is an independent, non‑partisan think tank focused on geopolitics and economics across Africa and the Middle East.

Mission

We aim to strengthen policy with rigorous analysis and pragmatic proposals, addressing challenges at the intersection of debt, climate, energy and governance.

Approach

Our work programs combine applied research with policy engagement and partnerships with academic institutions and policy labs across the region.

“Clarity, evidence and constructive dialogue can illuminate better policy choices.”

— Menara
  • Independent and evidence‑based
  • Networked with Global South partners
  • Accessible writing, timely briefs

What we do

Applied research and policy proposals, organized around three pillars.

Debt sustainability & restructuring

From architecture gaps to credit ratings and contingent risks.

Access to liquidity & long‑term finance

From SDRs to development finance reform and private capital leverage.

Governance & China as an international financier

State capacity and comparative insights on creditor institutions.
